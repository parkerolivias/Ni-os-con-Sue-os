Welcome to Niños con Sueños

We are a youth-driven nonprofit working to provide educational tools, creative opportunities, and life-changing resources to underprivileged children in Mexico. Join us in creating brighter futures, one child at a time.

About Us

Founded by Parker Schultz, Niños con Sueños began as a personal mission after volunteering with children in Mexico City. What started as a few reading and art classes has grown into a passionate initiative to support children from underserved communities with the essentials they need to learn, grow, and thrive.
